Pretoria

The capital is, across the country, the metro city with highest commuting times reported by black commuters.Analysing just those people who relies on not private transport, the average one way is more than 80 minutes.

This contrast with the reality reported by the sample of white commuters, who stated, in average a commuting time of 44 minutes.

The difference of the access for public transport, between different race groups is almost as higher as Cape Town's. While Black commuters states they need in average 11 minutes to reach the first transport mode, white commuters needs just 2.
